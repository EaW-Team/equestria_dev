If you're reading this, then you're most likely looking to impliment your own country selection screen map for EaW, or perhaps seeking inspiration for another mod. Either way, welcome!

https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1rakuJBqmSFsY1ANGFlxR-HXan6Fqz5gi?usp=drive_link
The three .psd files in this folder are what we used to create the maps you see in the country selection screen.
Full credit goes to Deer (lunar_explorator) for making the map. (If you're using these maps, please do make sure to credit him!)

Creating the glow/highlight effect revolves around the "Mask" folder and the "Name mask" layer in the layers list.
You're going to need to mask out the "map darken" and "map desaturate" layers as we have done in the .psd files to ensure that only your country is bright in the selection.
We also add an extra layer on top of these two effect layers by copying the border color of the country ("Border Colors" layer) and giving it a gaussian blur to give a faint glow to the country.
Make sure to also mask out the "Name mask" layer to ensure that only your country's text is bright.

After that, it is a simple matter of implementing the map under the template, adding a texticon definition, and placing the texticon into the appropriate 'brief history' localization key of your nation,
you can see how it is done in the loc files.

And you're all set! Have fun!
