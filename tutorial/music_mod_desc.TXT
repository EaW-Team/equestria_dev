The Equestria at War team is proud to present the Equestria at War music mod. Featuring 31 tracks from the My Little Pony fandom from such artists as Radiarc and Carbon Maestro.

New in the 1.5.2 beta patch: We have added a toggable option to the in-game music player that lets you to choose between vanilla songs, mod songs or both!

Note for YouTube: while we have permission to use the music in this mod, we cannot guarantee that it is YouTube friendly.
