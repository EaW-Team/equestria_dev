[h1]Equestria at War (EaW)[/h1] is a mod that tries to recreate the fantasy world of Equestria from My Little Pony franchise in a slightly darker setting with industrialization, corruption and political disagreements. The scenario focuses on global confrontation between Changelings and Equestria.

[img]https://i.imgur.com/LdUh4ni.png[/img]

+ Pleasant My Little Pony fantasy setting
+ New custom map based on the My Little Pony Universe
+ Over 100 immersive focus trees, allowing for many possibilities
+ Expanded technology trees. Extended tech branches, magical equipment
+ New technology trees with racial and magical abilities of the fantasy races
+ Thousands of events and event chains
+ All new artwork created for the mod and art from across the My Little Pony fandom
+ Ponyfied UI
+ Custom 3D models
+ Custom soundtrack, featuring multiple tracks made specially for the mod

[h1]“After the Storm” 2.6[/h1]
The Storm King, the Founder of the Storm Kingdom, the Uniter of Yetikind, the Conqueror of Zebrica, is dead.

Now vultures fight over what remains. Four ambitious yeti warlords have claimed the Horned Crown and are locked in a deadly struggle to seize the Storm King's inheritance for themselves. Ibyz Icepaw is one of these yetis, a Field Marshal experienced in both war and running an occupation from her work in the Zwahile. Having suddenly come out of retirement to throw her might into the ring and save the Rainband from greedy kleptocrats and madyetis. Seeking to build a kingdom her kind deserves and prove to the rest of the continent there is more to yetis then just mad conquest. It's up to Ibyz to see if she can fulfil her promises, especially as the more she focuses on achieving her goals the more the zebras of the Zwahile expect their overlord to not forget their compliance doesn't come for free.

In the ruins of their fallen Queendom, the hyenas gather under the guidance of Fahamka, the Great Teacher of the Qaylidi. Having carefully waited and watched ever since their homeland burned under the Storm King's advance, they ready themselves to strike and finally reclaim what is theirs. Their past was one of oppression and cruelty, their present is one of enslavement and struggle, but the future will be theirs to shape. And to the Qaylidi Dervishes, that is all that matters.

Far to the north, the war drums echo. After the Storm King destroyed the Imperial Mazwi, the Great Lakes are much like a burned down forest. Green sprouts are breaking through the ashes, growing quickly to seize the new opportunities. The Kingdom of Zar-Hay is one such sprout. Their royal family is missing, but an ambitious regent has taken the reins of leadership and has a plan to ensure his homeland will never be enslaved ever again. A plan that is meant to turn Zar-Hay into a mighty nation, strong enough to withstand any invader. A plan to create another empire to replace the Mazwi. After all, what was once divided, must unite.
