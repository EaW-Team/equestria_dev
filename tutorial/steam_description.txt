[h1]Equestria at War (EaW)[/h1] is a mod that tries to recreate the fantasy world of Equestria from My Little Pony franchise in a slightly darker setting with industrialization, corruption and political disagreements. The scenario focuses on global confrontation between Changelings and Equestria.

[img]https://i.imgur.com/LdUh4ni.png[/img]

[h1]“Scorched Earth” 2.5[/h1]

The kirin and zebras of Southeast Zebrica have long held grudges against each other, ever since settlers from the north began to settle the lands that today are known as Zaikiria. The Commandery of Zaikiria served as a unifying banner for all the independent city states, ensuring they would follow the guidance of Concord from the north. In response, mighty kingdoms formed across the Marekong to resist this influence, such as the Kingdom of Zongo, which once controlled the entire nearby basin. However, not all that glitters is gold, and the failures of centralisation and modernisation led both these powerful states to collapse into stagnation.

The spirit of the Commandery of Zaikiria remains alive within the Lan Kir Militarchy – the banners and vanguards that have long protected the frontiers of kirinity. Left alone in a sea of traitors, the Lan Kir nearly fell to anarchy as Vermillion too succumbed to corruption and decadence. With none to trust, the brave valiants protecting these frontiers centralised under the rule of the Primus Militarch, solidifying their devotion and continuing their duty to the Matriarch Superior and Concord. With Concord's blessing, they shall bring order to the pretenders who have for too long tarnished Zaikiria, and following the will of the Matriarch Superior and their Primus Militarch, they shall expand the cause of kirinity farther than it has ever reached.

Across the Marekong, the Kingdom of Zongo still reels from the recent conflict against Zeblu. Having long since left its golden age of influence, Zongo sits atop riches it cannot fully exploit. With the previous Mwenezongo having perished in the war, his wife, Queen-Mother Nzuzi, has done all she can to ensure the kingdom remains stable and prepared for the return of her son, Akida Bomboko, from his studies abroad. With Akida's graduation fast approaching, the prince's return heralds the ideas of modernity he experienced and embraced while abroad. The extent of his vision remains a mystery, but all in Zongo sense that change – for better or worse – is on the horizon.

Far from the shores of Zebrica, there is another Southeast where change is stirring. When Nightmare Moon returned and all hope seemed lost, many statelets rose to carve their own visions and hopes into the warring continent. Among these figures fighting for salvation, a better future, or simply their own independence, there is one who does not fit the mold. The notorious scoundrel and villain from the Daring Do series, Doctor Caballeron, arrives in the Southeast of Equestria with two aims: to make money and humiliate Daring Do – whatever happens to Aztlan is simply a side effect.

The Southeasts will soon find themselves facing great struggles. Will Lan Kir regain its glory and prove itself worthy of Concord's blessing, or will it collapse under the weight of its own fury? Will Zongo reform its tribal society into a bastion of modernity, or will it stagnate under the forces of reaction? Will Aztlan rise as a true bastion of freedom, or will its legacy be one of ambition and blood? The future of the Southeasts is yours to define!
